About Mae

I'm a graphic designer drawn to quiet details, intentional choices, and the spaces where clarity meets curiosity. My work often begins with a feeling — an idea that unfolds through research, intuition, and form.

I gravitate toward projects that involve storytelling, systems, and the subtleties of visual communication — inspired by the pace of print, the silence of structure, and the poetry of everyday things.
