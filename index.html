About

Design
as a felt
language.

I'm Mae Montero — a graphic and brand designer with a foundation in visual communication and a deep interest in how design creates meaning. My work spans brand identity, editorial systems, packaging, and motion, always built around clarity, intention, and atmosphere.

I bring a considered eye to every project — one that's shaped by a love of typography, the logic of layout, and the belief that the best design is felt before it's understood.
