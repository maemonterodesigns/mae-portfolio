About

Design
as a felt
language.

I'm Mae Montero, a designer drawn to quiet details, intentional choices, and the spaces where clarity meets curiosity. I'm most interested in the in-between: where structure becomes softness, where type turns into tone, where layout starts to feel like language.

My work often begins with a feeling — an idea that unfolds through research, intuition, and form. White space, rhythm, and restraint play a big role in how I build. I believe what's left unsaid can be just as powerful as what's shown.
