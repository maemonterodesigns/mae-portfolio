I'm Mae Montero, a designer drawn to quiet details, intentional choices, and the spaces where clarity meets curiosity. Where structure becomes softness, where type turns into tone, where layout starts to feel like language.  My work often begins with a feeling — an idea that unfolds through research, intuition, and form. White space, rhythm, and restraint play a big role in how I build.
